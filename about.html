ABOUT US
Great Empire Technologies Limited was established in the year 2008, invested in and organized by a team of security professionals. The company is incorporated with the Nigeria cooperate affairs commission,

Great Empire Technologies Limited which has over 5 branches nation-wide is the sole distributor / marketer of GETEL ranges of CCTV products which is manufactured with US standard and ISO certified.

The constant rise in crime rate and the quest for improved technology led to the birth of the company that is focused on home, office and industrial security, solar technologies, telecommunication, and general electrical services with a high technical and well trained team of experts, makes Great Empire Technologies Limited a force to identify with in everyday technology and with a passion to satisfy its numerous clients with a friendly and courteous services makes us outstanding and the best of its kind.
