About Our Company

Great Empire Technologies Limited was established in the year 2008, invested in and organized by a team of security professionals. The company is incorporated with the Nigeria cooperate affairs commission,

Great Empire Technologies Limited which has over 5 branches nation-wide isthe sole distributor / marketer of GETEL ranges of CCTV products which is manufactured with US standard and ISO certified.

The constant rise in crime rate and the quest for improved technology led to the birth of the company that is focused on home, office and industrial security, solar technologies, telecommunication, and general electrical services with a high technical and well trained team of experts, makes Great Empire Technologies Limited a force to identify with in everyday technology and with a passion to satisfy its numerous clients with a friendly and courteous services makes us outstanding and the best of its kind.

OVERVIEW

With the advancement of technology in the area of crime detection and monitoring, the need for CCTV applications has become undeniable, which is why we are introducing the Closed Circuit Television system (CCTV) as a tool in tracking crimes and monitoring movement in public places and private companies The technology is simple to use and enables the management/organization to view, monitor, reduce and manage situational crimes. With our comprehensive and fully customized reports,the utilization of this system is made easy.
